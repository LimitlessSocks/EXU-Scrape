Digital Bugs

Sock (LimitlessSocks)

X cards
Digital Bugs is somewhat infamous for being a very Table 500 strategy with the capacity to surprise. The custom support attempts to capture that surprise and flesh it out into a fledgling strategy. The deck has received two pieces of direct custom support: Digital Bug TaruntuRAM (which is Resistrider before it was cool), and Digital Bug Locathode, which is a Link-2 which can inherently use a Digital Bug in hand as material, ensuring you have at least some archetypal turn 1 plays, even if you brick. Features Galaxy Soldier as GY setup and a free disruption, and is heavier on the handtrap side. Drossamer Bagworm is a piece of indirect custom support, being a free LIGHT Level 3 Insect that changes battle positions.
