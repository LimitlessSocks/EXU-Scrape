Dragon Paladins

Ani (T.A.P.)

X cards
Dragon Paladins, otherwise known as the Paladins of [Adjective] Dragons, are a series of cards based on Level 4 Ritual Monsters that are meant to be alternative tech options for ease-of-access to certain cards based on that Paladin's steed. With support from ExaltedDawn, they are able to stand as a Deck all their own, with very easy access to the Rank 4 toolbox on turn 1, and formidable Battle Phases on later turns.
