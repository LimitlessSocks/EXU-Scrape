Azuroth the Unholy Light/Azuroth the Demon King

How to Play!

  1. "Azuroth the Demon King" enters the Duel with 4000 LP, and uses its own Deck as shown.
    1. It is recommended that if a player has or can become "Azuroth the Demon King" that they use the "Azuroth the Demon King" card to represent as such, if available.
  2. When a player becomes "Azuroth the Demon King", they banish all cards that "Azuroth the Demon King" does not own, face-down.
    1. If necessary, a player can instead use cards they own to represent cards that "Azuroth the Demon King" owns by denoting as such.
      1. A player cannot have at least 2 copies of any 1 card represent 2 different cards.
  3. "Azuroth the Demon King" enters the Duel by shuffling the Deck it owns and drawing 4 cards. It then immediately starts its turn.

Clarifying Rules for Competitive(?) Play

  1. A player can start the Duel as "Azuroth the Demon King" outside of a competitive setting if all players agree.
    1. More than 1 player can become "Azuroth the Demon King".
      1. Players do not share Decks if multiple players become "Azuroth the Demon King".
    2. A player cannot become "Azuroth the Demon King" unless a card or effect allows them to do so.
    3. If all players agree to the following at the start of the Match, it may apply: "If a player that has become "Azuroth the Demon King" is attacked by a player equipped with "Benevolence of the Demonsbane" and their LP becomes 0, they lose the Match."
  2. If a Chain is resolving and a player loses the Duel before the effect of "Azuroth the Unholy Light" would apply to them, they do not become "Azuroth the Demon King".
  3. A player that has become "Azuroth the Demon King" is still a player.
    1. A player that has become "Azuroth the Demon King" cannot lose the Duel by drawing with no cards in their Deck.
    2. A player that has become "Azuroth the Demon King" still loses the Duel by their opponent's effects that win the Duel.
