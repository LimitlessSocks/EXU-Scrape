Lenguini, the Imprisoned

How to Play!

  1. "Lenguini the Imprisoned" enters the Duel with 8000 and uses the Deck as shown.
  2. When "Lenguini, the Imprisoned" is successfully Special Summoned as a player, Lenguini's owner will randomly choose 5 cards from their decklist to be their opening hand.
  3. Lenguini uses a seperate field for their cards, Lenguini's Extra Monster Zones cannot be used by other players.
  4. Lenguini's turn begins after the end of their owner's turn.
  5. "Lenguini, the Imprisoned" can perform mandatory trigger effects and mandatory game actions (such as drawing for your normal draw during the Draw Phase.) For optional actions, any other players present in the Duel may perform 1 activated effect or game action during each of lenguini's phases. Once each player has performed any desired actions/effects, the turn continues to the next phase.
  6. While Lenguini occupies its owners Extra Monster Zone, they may not summon any monsters to an empty Extra Monster Zone.
  7. If the LP of "Lenguini, the imprisoned" reach 0, all other players go into mourning, and lose the Duel, therefore, Lenguini is the winner of that Duel. This is not considered a card effect.
  8. Lenguini is not treated as a card after being successfully Special Summoned as a player.
