EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Genshutsu

Gatr

Genshutsu is a monster-only Ritual Deck that uses Ritual monsters to Ritual Summon. They are a tempo-focused deck that excels at going 2nd and breaking boards, then out resourcing the opponent. Each Ritual has a removal effect on Summon, and the deck is capable of Summoning them as a Quick Effect.
