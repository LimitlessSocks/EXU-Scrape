EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Tsurumashi

Mevs

Part of a combined effort to introduce the new Type "Yokai", as well as the type-changing clause (This monster's original Type is treated as Yokai rather than TYPE.). A Ritual Control deck that aims to bring out 1 or both of its Rituals turn 1 maybe with a Set card or 2. It uses "Kami Tokens" to turbo out said Rituals, also it truly uses the GY as a resource for effects by shuffling them into the Deck. You will likely have little cards in hand at the end of the turn and you are fine with that.
