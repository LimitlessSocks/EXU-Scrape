EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Flamiller

DrawingCards

FIRE Reptile deck that tries to mill the opponent but not as it's primary win-con. The entire deck revolves around Flamiller Serpent, which mills 3 from the opponent's deck and summons a monster from your hand when sent from the field to the GY (non-hopt, please do not insta deny because of this). The deck plays as a scaling deck that forces the opponent to manage the amount of cards in their GY.
