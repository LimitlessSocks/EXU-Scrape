EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Animathos

Domedread

Animathos, the made up word, is formed from the Greek words “Anima” and “Ethos”, and means “To appeal to the ethics of the soul”. Animathos, the archetype, is a primarily going second Fusion deck that is all about sending fusion monsters from the extra deck to the GY, and then their archetypal fusions can contact fuse from the GY.
