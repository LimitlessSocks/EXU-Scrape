EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Custom Query Reference

In general, you can think of using the Custom Query like writing PSCT for a card. Case does not matter! Here are some examples:

  • light or dark dragon shows all monsters whose Type is Dragon, and that are LIGHT and/or DARK attribute.
  • custom trap shows all custom Trap Cards.
  • extra "fur hire" shows all "Fur Hire" Extra Deck monsters.
  • level 4 or lower warrior main shows all Level 4 or lower Warrior Main Deck monsters (aka Reinforcement of the Army targets).
  • (dark fiend) or (light pyro) shows all DARK Fiend monsters and/or LIGHT Pyro monsters.
  • continuous spell/trap shows all Continuous Spells and Continuous Traps.
  • 3400 atk or def shows all monsters with 3400 ATK, and/or with 3400 DEF.
  • 3400 atk/def shows all monsters with 3000 ATK and DEF.
  • level 4 fire fiend shows all Level 4 FIRE Fiend monsters.
  • monster not earth shows all monsters that are not EARTH monsters.
  • not earth shows all cards that are not EARTH monsters, meaning it will also turn up Spells and Traps!
  • level 3, 6, or 9 synchro level 3,6,9 synchro both show all Synchro Monsters whose Level is 3, 6, or 9.
  • monster @atk<def@ shows all monsters whose ATK is strictly less than their DEF.
  • monster @atk % 100 /= 0@ shows all monsters whose ATK is not a multiple of 100.
