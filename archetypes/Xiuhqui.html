EXU: Extinction Unleashed

Xiuhqui

Gatr

A Fusion Pendulum Deck that aims to first place Fusions from the face-down Extra Deck into the face-up Extra Deck, then place them into the scales. It grinds via Pendulum Summoning a single Main Deck monster and using its effect to Fusion Summon. It gets better over time, and all the Fusions can be quite disruptive.
